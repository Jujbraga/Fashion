Style Coaching

Coaching and Style Coaching

As an accredited coach, trainer and mediator with many years of experience I help clients achieve their personal or professional goals through guidance and supporting advices. In my opinion we can combine my profession with the word of fashion and clothes to provide a more complex service.

I can not only help you with styling tips and outfit suggestions; we can also work on your mind and well-being. I can help boost your confidence from the inside as well as from the outside.

I can help you match your personality with your wardrobe and overcome difficulties in your personal life. All through clothes!

I think it is especially important for us women to take a little time out for ourselves and nurture our souls. In this way you can get back to your daily life recharged and in balance with yourself.
