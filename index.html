Welcome!

My name is Krisztina Kukity, coach, stylist and personal shopper from Budapest.

I have always loved fashion and clothes were my favourite way to express myself. Over time it became my passion to help and show others how the right clothes can change their life.

I also love second hand shops, because I always find unique pieces at a surprisingly good price. Not to mention how it helps the environment to not shop at fast fashion stores. Here on my page you can find information about the movement of slow fashion and why it is a more conscious way of shopping.

Combining my love of styling clothes and slow fashion birthed the idea to create this website where I can show You how to do it. How to dress from second hand stores and how to shop our own closets.
